Uncover the Potential of Personalized Banking at Senkadagala Cooperative Savings Bank

Senkada Business Development Cooperative Bank began its journey as a state-owned Licensed Specialized Bank in July 2006 after being established in accordance with the Banking Act and incorporated in accordance with the Companies Act.

We take great satisfaction in offering premium financial products and services that are also competitively priced and creative. These services are made available through channels that cater to customers, are supported by cutting-edge technology, and are provided by a varied group of professionals who collaborate well. Our goal is to offer complete solutions to all of our stakeholders while fostering a culture of ongoing learning and value development.
